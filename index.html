Research

My current masters reserach project is looking at the injective envelope of operator spaces and C*-envelopes of operator algebras using boundary representations. I am interested in injectivity for commutative C*-algebras. Equivalently using Gelfand duality projectivity for compact Hausdorff spaces.

We can consider a topological group acting continuously on these compact Hausdorff spaces, the so called G-flows. I am also interested in studying projectivity in the category of these G-flows with morphisms given as G-equivariant continuous maps.

Jashan Bal

I am currently a PhD student in Pure Mathematics at the University of Waterloo under the supervision of Matthew Kennedy and Andy Zucker. My research interests include dynamics and its various interactions with different areas of math.

I completed my masters at the University of Waterloo under the supervision of Laurent Marcoux. My masters research project was a survey on the C*-envelope using the techniques of injectivity and boundary representations. You can obtain a copy of the paper here.
